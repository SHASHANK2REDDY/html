It is important for children to be told and read such stories, so the story of the Rabbit and the tortoise is very popular among children. If you want to inculcate understanding in your kids, then telling them moral stories is a great option. That’s why we bring you this short story on the morality of the Rabbit and the tortoise today, which shows important values and morals.
